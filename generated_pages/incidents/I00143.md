# Incident I00143: Article claiming Texas declared Pride flags in classrooms illegal is satirical

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: The US state of Texas has declared that bringing an LGBTQ pride flag into a classroom is a crime.<br><br>Our verdict: The article which originally published the claim is satirical. This isn’t real legislation in Texas.</i>
