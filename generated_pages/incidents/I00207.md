# Incident I00207: Fact check: Claim comparing cost of border wall to HealthCare.gov misattributed to actor Tim Allen

* **Summary:** <i>The claim: Actor Tim Allen said the Obamacare website cost more than the border wall<br><br>[...]<br><br>A viral meme appearing to quote actor Tim Allen saying, "President Trump’s Wall Costs Less Than The Obamacare Website. Let that sink in, America," is FALSE, based on our research. Allen did not author this quote, according to the actor's publicist. Also, estimates put the cost of Trump's border wall project significantly higher than the cost of HealthCare.gov.</i>
