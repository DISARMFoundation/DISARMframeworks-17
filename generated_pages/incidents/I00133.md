# Incident I00133: ‘Dead Gazan body miraculously texting’ is actually Thai child in Halloween costume

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: A picture has been taken in Gaza of a child wearing a body bag having pretended to be dead.<br><br>Our verdict: False. The photo is of a child in a costume at a Halloween competition in Thailand in 2022</i>
