# Incident I00131: Video shows scenes in Algiers, not bombing in Gaza

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: The video shows the Gaza Strip following the recent conflict between Israel and Hamas.<br><br>Our verdict: The footage has been located to Algiers, Algeria, and was online before 7 October 2023. It’s likely to show football celebrations, which can be seen in many similar videos.</i>
