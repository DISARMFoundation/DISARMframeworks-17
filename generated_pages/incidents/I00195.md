# Incident I00195: Fact check: False quote attributed to Michael Kors about African Americans 

* **Summary:** <i>Social media users have been sharing images online with a quote attributed to designer Michael Kors that says, “I’m tired of pretending that I like blacks.” This claim is false. [...] The fabricated quote is from a now-deleted satirical website.</i>
