# Incident I00224: Photofake: Anti-Ukrainian Billboards Appear in Poland

* **Summary:** <i>The photograph circulated by Russian media and pro-Kremlin social media users as proof of anti-Ukrainian advertising in Poland, is an example of basic photoshop manipulation. There are scores of identical photos on the web which containe different advertisements on the billboards.</i>
