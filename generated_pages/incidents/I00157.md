# Incident I00157: Edited video of Bihar politician Tejashwi Yadav used to claim he was ‘drunk’ during media briefing

* **Summary:** <i>A video of RJD leader Tejashwi Yadav has been edited to falsely suggest he was addressing reporters in an intoxicated state. We found that the original video was manipulated by slowing it down to support this claim.</i>
