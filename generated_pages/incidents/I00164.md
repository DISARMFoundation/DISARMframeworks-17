# Incident I00164: Video of ‘actor in Gaza limping on the wrong foot’ is from comedy skit filmed in Iraq

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: A video shows an actor in Gaza who accidentally uses the wrong foot while limping, implying the injury is fake.<br><br>Our verdict: This isn't what it shows. The video is a comedy skit filmed in Baghdad, Iraq, and is not related to the current Middle East conflict.</i>
