# Incident I00142: Picture of Bono and Bob Geldof holding Israeli flags is AI-generated

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: A picture shows Bono and Bob Geldof holding Israeli flags and staging a “two-man vigil” outside the Israeli embassy in Dublin.<br><br>Our verdict: This picture isn’t real. It was generated by artificial intelligence.</i>
