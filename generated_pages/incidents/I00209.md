# Incident I00209: Fact check: False excerpt from Trump’s ‘Art of the Deal’ on ‘never admitting defeat’

* **Summary:** <i>Users on social media are sharing a purported excerpt from “Trump: Art of the Deal”, a book authored by U.S. President Donald Trump and Tony Schwartz that was published in 1987. The alleged excerpt shows Trump describing his approach on “never admitting defeat”. This is false. This quotation is not part of the “Art of the Deal.</i>
