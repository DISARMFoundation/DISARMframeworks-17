# Incident I00149: Fact Check: Clip of UFOs in Nevada created with simulation game

* **Summary:** <i>Footage from a video game has been miscaptioned as saying it shows UFOs flying over Nevada. Other online posts use the clip to fuel a conspiracy called Project Blue Beam, which says the U.S. government falsifies UFO footage.</i>
