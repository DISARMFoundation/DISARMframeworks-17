# Incident I00145: Elon Musk has not agreed to buy CNN for $3 billion

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: Elon Musk has agreed to buy CNN for $3 billion.<br><br>Our verdict: This is not true. The claim comes from a satirical article and CNN has confirmed the network is not being sold or changing ownership.</i>
