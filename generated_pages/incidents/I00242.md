# Incident I00242: Billy Dee Williams Says His Facebook Page Got Hacked and Began Posting Charlie Kirk Disinformation. Millions of People Were Fooled

* **Summary:** <i>Facebook post data compiled by PressProgress shows that over a three-day period between September 10 and September 13, Williams’ official verified Facebook page made 66 posts in reference to Charlie Kirk. These posts were collectively shared over 70,000 times and received over 1.6 million likes and other reactions.<br><br>[...]<br><br>Williams, best known to audiences for his iconic role as Star Wars’ Lando Calrissian, announced Sunday that he had been hacked and that a number of recent politically-charged posts on his page were not his own.</i>
