# Incident I00194: Fact check: False quote attributed to Nelson Mandela

* **Summary:** <i>Social media users have been sharing a meme featuring a picture of British government front-bench ministers alongside a quote on nationalisation attributed to Nelson Mandela. There is no evidence Mandela ever made the statement quoted.</i>
