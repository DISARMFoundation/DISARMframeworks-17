# Incident I00203: Fact check: Viral post backing Trump came from imposter Hallie Biden account

* **Summary:** <i>The claim: Hallie Biden said Donald Trump won the 2020 election <br><br>[...]<br><br>Based on our research, we rate FALSE the claim that Hallie Biden said Trump won the 2020 election. A spokesperson for Hallie Biden said the post comes from an imposter account, not from her directly. The account has since been suspended.</i>
