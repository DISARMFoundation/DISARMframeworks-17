# Incident I00244: Hackers publish fake story about Ukrainians attempting to assassinate Slovak president

* **Summary:** <i>An unidentified attacker hacked a Czech news service's website and published a fake story on Tuesday claiming that an assassination attempt had been made against the newly elected Slovak president, Peter Pellegrini.</i>
