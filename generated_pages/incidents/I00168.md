# Incident I00168: Video does not show a Palestinian woman saying ‘we’re prisoners of Hamas’

* **Summary:** <i>Fake subtitles have been added to footage circulating on social media to suggest a Palestinian woman said in Arabic “we are prisoners of Hamas”. However, this is not a correct translation. </i>
