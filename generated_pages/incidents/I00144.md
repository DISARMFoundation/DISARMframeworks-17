# Incident I00144: Viral Kamala Harris-attributed ‘vengeance’ quote is from 2019 satirical news article

* **Summary:** <i>What was claimed: In 2020 Kamala Harris said that once Democrats were re-elected to the White House, Donald Trump supporters would “feel the vengeance of a nation”.<br><br>Our verdict: She never said this. The quote originates from a satirical news website.</i>
