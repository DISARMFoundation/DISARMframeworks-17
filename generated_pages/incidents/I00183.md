# Incident I00183: Fact Check: Antarctic ice data cherry-picked to make false global warming claim

* **Summary:** <i>A misleading comparison of Antarctic sea ice levels on two specific days 27 years apart has been used by people online to falsely claim that climate change warnings are a scam.</i>
