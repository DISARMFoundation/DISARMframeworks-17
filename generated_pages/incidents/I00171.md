# Incident I00171: FALSE: Angel Locsin ‘tests positive’ for coronavirus

* **Summary:** <i>Claim: Actress Angel Locsin “tested positive” for the coronavirus after conducting several relief operations.<br><br>[...]<br><br>The facts: On April 26, the day the links were first shared on Facebook, Locsin announced that she had tested negative for the coronavirus. GMA News Online reported it the same day.</i>

We urge the public to practice responsible use of social media and refrain from sharing such items that only encourage the spread of misinformation,” it said.<br><br>In the original video uploaded by the official YouTube channel of GMA Public Affairs, Araullo talked about two new cases of COVID-19 in the country, but neither of them was Locsin.
