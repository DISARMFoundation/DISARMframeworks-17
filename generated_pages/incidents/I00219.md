# Incident I00219: Fact Check-BBC did not report Poland preparing to send troops to Ukraine

* **Summary:** <i>A clip that purports to show content published by the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) reporting that Poland is preparing to send troops to Ukraine is digitally altered and was not published by the outlet.</i>
