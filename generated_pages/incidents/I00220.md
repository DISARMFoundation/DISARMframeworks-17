# Incident I00220: Fact Check: Fake BBC clip on Ukrainian politician selling arms to Hamas

* **Summary:** <i>The BBC did not publish a video claiming that a Ukrainian politician sold arms to Hamas as suggested by images circulating online that carry BBC branding and cite investigative group Bellingcat. The clip is fake, according to representatives of both BBC and Bellingcat.</i>
