# Technique T0048.011: Content Depicting Physical Sexual Violence

**Summary**: Content qualifies as depicting physical sexual violence when posted or streamed material shows, suggests, or meaningfully represents non-consensual sexual acts or sexual harm involving real individuals. <br><br>Publishing such material can revictimise survivors by re-exposing them to the assault, enabling further circulation, and amplifying the emotional, psychological, and social impact of the violence.<br><br>Analysts should determine that the content clearly portrays physical sexual violence, assess whether consent is absent or impossible.

**Tactic**: TA18 Drive Online Harms <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0048 Harass
