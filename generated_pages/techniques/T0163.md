# Technique T0163: Issues with Cited Academic Research

**Summary**: A narrative makes a claim, and points to academic research to back up the legitimacy of the claim - however, the research has issues which make it an unreliable source.<br><br>This Technique contains Sub-Techniques which identify specific issues with cited academic research.<br><br>To count as a citation, the content must explicitly point to a specific piece of academic research to support a claim. This could be via an embedded link, footnotes, or providing the name of a paper, the journal it appears in, and the researchers who produced it.

**Tactic**: TA14 Develop Narratives
