# Technique T0048.010: Threaten Physical Sexual Violence

**Summary**: Content qualifies as a threat of physical sexual violence when it communicates, implicitly or explicitly, an intention to inflict sexual harm or coerced sexual acts on a specific individual. Threat actors may use such threats to intimidate or compel targets into taking or avoiding certain actions by invoking credible offline sexual harm. Threats of physical sexual violence is a form of harassment often targeting women online. <br><br>Analysts should confirm that the published content could reasonably be interpreted as a threat of sexual violence by its intended recipient.

**Tactic**: TA18 Drive Online Harms <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0048 Harass
