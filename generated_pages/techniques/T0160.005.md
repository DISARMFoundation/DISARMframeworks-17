# Technique T0160.005: Content Produced as Satire

**Summary**: Satirical content often mimics real events or personas for comedic or critical effect. While not intended to deceive, satire can be mistaken for factual reporting when shared without context. <br><br>Indicators include hyperbole, comedic tone, or origin from known satire outlets. Fact-checkers often verify such content as false but satirical.

**Tactic**: TA14 Develop Narratives <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0160 Content Verifiability
