# Technique T0165.001: Clipped Content

**Summary**: This sub-technique can be used to document audio or video content which is a segment taken from a longer piece of content. For example, a 20 second video which was produced by taking a short section out of a five minute video would be considered clipped content.<br><br>Actors have been observed clipping media in such a way that removes its original context, allowing for a creation of a new narrative.<br><br>To make this assessment, analysts will need to find the original unedited content, and identify the segment from which the clipped content was produced.

**Tactic**: TA06 Develop Content <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0165 Edited Content
