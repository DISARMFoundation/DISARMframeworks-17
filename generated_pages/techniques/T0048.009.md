# Technique T0048.009: Threaten Physical Violence

**Summary**: Content qualifies as a threat of physical violence when it communicates, implicitly or explicitly, the intent to cause bodily harm to a specific individual. Threat actors may use such threats to coerce targets into taking or avoiding certain actions by invoking credible offline harm. <br><br>Analysts should confirm that the published content could reasonably be interpreted as a physical threat by its intended recipient.

**Tactic**: TA18 Drive Online Harms <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0048 Harass
