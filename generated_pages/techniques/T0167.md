# Technique T0167: Issue with Content's Headline

**Summary**: This Technique contains Sub-Techniques which allow analysts to identify issues with how a piece of content has been titled.<br><br>Users of social media platforms often do not click in to view online content, and as such commonly only see its’ headline, where effectively experience the headline of the article as the whole article. In cases where a screenshot of a headline is posted, users are unable to view the rest of the content for context. Because of this, issues with content’s headlines can easily mislead people online.

**Tactic**: TA14 Develop Narratives
