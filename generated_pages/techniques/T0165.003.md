# Technique T0165.003: Playback Speed Altered

**Summary**: This sub-technique covers undisclosed speeding up or slowing down of Audio or Video Content prior to publication.<br><br>Actors have been observed slowing down footage of targeted speakers, making their words sound slurred, enabling narratives questioning the health of the target, or their state of mind.<br><br>To make this assessment, analysts will need to find the original unedited content, and pair sections of the published materials to sections of the original content.

**Tactic**: TA06 Develop Content <br><br>**Parent Technique:** T0165 Edited Content
